Urska

She is a dog. She is our dog.

We got Urska from the Humane Society about 10 years ago.
When we first met her we assumed she was more Black Lab vs German Shepard because she was so friendly with us.
WRONG. Within 2 weeks of staying with us, she considered our home her territory, which she ferociously gaurded.
She was incredibly athletic and loyal to us, which was great...unless other people wanted to interact with us.
It was difficult to have people over to our house. Kids were out of the question.
She finally started slowing down about two years ago, and has mellowed.
While she will still let out a throaty bark anytime someone approaches the house, she now will tolerate visitors, in between naps.

Her favorite things:

  • chasing cats, squirles, or any other moving animal
  • any kind of tablescraps
  • maintaining a secure perimeter
  • anytime we leave butter/any other food on the counter

Things she hates:

  • strangers inside the house
  • strangers outside of the house
  • strangers nowhere near the house, but getting up in her personal space
  • swimming. maybe she's doesn't have any lab in her after all
